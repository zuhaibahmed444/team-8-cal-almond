Our Roots

3 Generations
Rooted in California.

The Buenaventura family journey began on the Oregon Trail. Since the 1800s, we have been farming in the San Joaquin Valley, surviving droughts and changing climates by adapting and respecting the land.

Today, brothers Bill and Matt oversee 1,000 acres in Modesto. Instead of selling out to massive conglomerates, we focus on smart micro-irrigation and protecting our local bee colonies. It’s a legacy of sustainable agriculture, passed down to fuel the next generation.
